Declare an enum Currency.

It should include the following currency codes (according to ISO 4217):

  • USD - United States dollar
  • EUR - Euro
  • GBP - Pound sterling
  • RUB - Russian ruble
  • UAH - Ukrainian hryvnia
  • KZT - Kazakhstani tenge
  • CAD - Canadian dollar
  • JPY - Japanese yen
  • CNY - Chinese yuan

You must include all of the above codes, others do not. The constant in the enum can be declared in any order.
